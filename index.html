Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the battery last?

The boAt Storm Call 3 Plus offers up to 7 days of battery life with normal usage including health monitoring. With heavy usage including Bluetooth calling and Always-On Display, you can expect around 2 days of battery life.

How can I customize the watch face?

The watch supports DIY Watch Face Studio, allowing you to create custom watch faces. You can also choose from multiple pre-installed watch faces through the Crest App on your smartphone.

How many sports modes are available?

The boAt Storm Call 3 Plus features an impressive 700+ sports modes including running, cycling, yoga, swimming, badminton, cricket, and many more activities to track your fitness journey comprehensively.

How is the call quality during Bluetooth calling?

The watch features Bluetooth 5.2 connectivity with built-in microphone and speaker providing crystal-clear call quality. You can make and receive calls, access contacts, and use the dial pad directly from your wrist.

What warranty is provided?

boAt provides a standard 1-year warranty on the Storm Call 3 Plus covering manufacturing defects. The warranty covers hardware issues but excludes damage due to misuse, water damage beyond IP67 rating, or physical damage.

Is it compatible with both Android and iOS?

Yes, the boAt Storm Call 3 Plus is compatible with both Android and iOS devices. Download the Crest App from Google Play Store or Apple App Store to sync your watch and access all features.
